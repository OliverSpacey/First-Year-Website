Our mission:

Our mission at DAD is simple, we want to deliver quality, fun, and engaging games and puzzles for children worldwide. To achieve this, we take full advatange of our team of experts in their respective fields (information on the team can be found on 'About us', or by clicking here).


Games and puzzles:

Our games and puzzles are the perfect learning tool for any child, and have been carefully and lovingly crafted by our team to be fun and practical!

We believe companies like ourselves should ensure that both they, and the content they produce, is universally recognised, free to use, and trustworthy. Without these key characteristics, how can there be any certainty that your child is getting the tools they need? That is why we are proud to say we are all of those and more! Our team works day and night to create fun, engaging, and useful learning tools for children of all ages. One of these can be found in the 'Game' section from the navigation bar, sidebar, or by clicking here.
