DAD wins award

DAD has been awarded the 'Best Children's Game Company of The Year' award, adding another commendation to our list! This comes after DAD spent the year working entirely from home, with no contact with anyone not in their individual bubbles. The year has been incredibly tough for everyone and this award helps recognise the incredible work our team has done over the last 12 months, and demonstrates our committment to providing quality experiences no matter the circumstances.

CEO visits children in Ghana

Earlier this year our CEO, Oliver Spacey, visited a small village in Ghana where he handed out iPads to the children there in an effort to aid their ability to use virtual learning tools. This came after children across the world were denied true educational experiences as a result of covid, with many children missing entire years worth of work. Oliver hoped that by providing these children with iPads, they would be able to work from home and make up for the work they had missed. Tablets have also given these children the opportunity enhance the learning they were recieving previously, adding many more elements to it through a virtual environment.

Major office relocation

The main office for DAD has been officially moved from our previous address in Wellingborough to a new one in a small village in Yorkshire. The office is actually a new house! It's the old vicarage of the town, and so holds some significance in the local community. But, the opportunity to use such a beautiful and remote location with so much history as our base of operations was too great to pass up. We are certain that this new location will provide us with abundant opportunity to create the great games and experiences we always have, but now with the added charm of the English countryside in them all!
