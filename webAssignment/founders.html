Meet the team!

Founder/CEO

As early as the age of 12 Oliver was helping his younger brothers with their school work, often acting as their tutor. This was the earliest example of Oliver's desire to help those he could, and sparked his love for tutoring. In the years that followed, Oliver would spend much of his time aiding his brothers and helping them understand their work, making every effort to ensure the work was as interesting as possible.

Media manager

Meet Diane Murdoch, our media manger. She spent many years as a social media manager at companies like google and apple before coming to DAD with the hopes of using her expertise to help children. Her unique way of managing social media allows for effective engagement with the userbase and ensures a constant line of communication between DAD and those we serve to help.

Lead programmer

John spent much of his childhood working on his father's computer, playing around with whatever he could. From as early as he could read and write he was programming as much as his imagination would allow. Since John developed his skills for programming he has been making games for his friends, always with fun in mind and never taking himself too seriously. This allowed him to gain a special talent for making engaging and honest-feeling games, suitable for children of any age.
